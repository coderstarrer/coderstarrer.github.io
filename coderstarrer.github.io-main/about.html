What is PlanckBot?

PlanckBot: Your Ultimate Discord Companion

PlanckBot was meticulously crafted to elevate your Discord experience, seamlessly blending powerful features with user-friendly tools. Developed by a team of passionate and dedicated developers, this bot strives to create a vibrant, safe, and engaging environment for users of all backgrounds. At the heart of PlanckBot's design lies its commitment to enhancing community interactions. With a robust profanity filter that encompasses nearly 5000 words, it ensures that conversations remain respectful and enjoyable. Users can engage freely, knowing that PlanckBot is there to keep the chat clean without stifling fun. Beyond moderation, PlanckBot introduces exciting elements to your server.
